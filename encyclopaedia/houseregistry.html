The Hei of Abaddi is the youngest house of nobility within Egypt. Only recently granted the status of nobles by Pharaoh Iahotep after a great victory over Bedoan raiders on the western border by General Sethtah, the family holds no special privileges or powers over any land as yet. Instead, they look to build their support and reputation in the Council, taking military campaigns wherever possible and supporting the Pharaoh and his Queen in order to win the favouritism necessary to advance and gain further glory for the Abaddi name, securing the future of its children. Standards of Practice
PoliticsThe members of the Abaddi family play the game and they play it well, with a demure dignity. They do not rush, they do not fight and they offer reason where anger might rule. They present themselves as self-assured but are always aware of their youth as a noble Hei and how every step is precious.
